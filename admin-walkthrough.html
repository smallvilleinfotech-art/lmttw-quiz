Admin Guide

Hosting Guide

How to build quizzes, run live games, and manage your community in Lead Me To The Waters.

Scroll
Step One

Build your quiz

In the Quizzes tab, add a name, then build out each question — options, the correct answer, a time limit, and points awarded.

1
Step Two

Open Live Host

Head to Live Host, choose the quiz you want to run, and tap Host Live to generate a fresh 6‑digit PIN for this session.

2
Step Three

Manage the lobby

Share the PIN out loud or on screen. Watch players' names appear live as they join, and start the game once enough are in.

3
Step Four

Launch each question

Questions display with a countdown. Watch the live answer bars fill in as players respond, right up until time runs out.

4
Step Five

Reveal the podium

The correct answer highlights, and the live top‑5 podium appears for everyone — your screen and every player's screen — instantly.

5
Step Six

Fine-tune the game

Open Settings to randomize question or answer order, switch sound effects and lobby music on or off, or enable autoplay to host hands‑free.

Sound effects
Countdown ticks, correct/wrong chimes
Background music
Lobby music while players join
6
Step Seven

Wrap up the game

After the final question, results save automatically. Review scores anytime in Reports or the Leaderboard tab.

7
Ongoing

Manage your community

Approve new contestants, keep an eye on flagged duplicate accounts, and export bank details for prize disbursement from the admin dashboard.

8
What Players See When You Host

Your PIN brings the room together

Share it once — players fill the lobby in real time.
