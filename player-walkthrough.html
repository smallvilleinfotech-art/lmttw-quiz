Player Guide

How to Play

Everything you need to join a game, answer fast, and climb the podium in Lead Me To The Waters.

Scroll
Get In

Enter the game

On the home screen, type the 6‑digit Game PIN your host shares and tap Join — or sign in and pick a quiz to play solo anytime.

1
Show Up

Choose your name

Pick a display name so everyone — including you — can spot your spot on the leaderboard once the game gets going.

2
Hang Tight

Wait in the lobby

Watch fellow players pop in one by one while gentle lobby music plays in the background. Your host controls when the game begins.

Lobby music preview
3
Move Fast

Answer each question

Read the question, then tap an answer before time runs out. Answering correctly — and quickly — earns you more points.

4
Stay Sharp

Follow your live score

Your score and rank update right on your screen after every question, so you always know exactly where you stand.

5
After Every Question

Watch the podium

The top 5 players appear on a live podium after each question — on your screen too, not just the host's — so you can see exactly who's climbing.

6
The Finish

See the final results

When the last question wraps up, the final podium reveals the winners. Check your placement and total score before heading back.

7
Anytime

Visit your dashboard

Review your past scores, accuracy, and where you rank on the global leaderboard — and jump into a solo quiz whenever you like.

8
The Moment It's All For

Everyone sees the podium

After each question, this updates live on your screen — not just the host's.
